Service Level Agreement (SLA) – GitDenGas

Service Provider: GitDenGas
Client: EK
Project/Service: Gitdengas (search engine)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Purpose
This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the commitments, service expectations, and operational standards for Gitdengas search engine. The purpose is to make clear rules and expectations, between GitDenGas and EK about perfomance, support, communication and measurable data.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. Scope of Services

Deliverables
- Monthly analytic reports
- bug fixes
- feature enhancements
- Site perfomance reports
- Site performance optimization 
- Logging of possible errors 
- Monitization of website usage (tracking usage)

Features
- Functioning search engine 
- Login function
- Register function
- Weatherpage
- About page

Excluded services
- Integration of 3rd party unsupported tools
- Hardware infrastructure costs

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. Service Availability

Availability targets
- 95% monthly uptime (early-stage development target)
- Suppoer for standard issues monday-friday 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM (CET)
- Support for critical issues (example complete server error) 24/7

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Support & Response Times

Priority levels, response time and target for resolution

| Priority          | Description (with examples)                                                  | Initial Response Time | Target Resolution      |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| **P1 – Critical** | Full platform outage                                                         | ≤ **1 hour**          | ≤ **8 hours**          |
| **P2 – High**     | Major feature malfunction                                                    | ≤ **4 hours**         | ≤ **24 hours**         |
| **P3 – Medium**   | Non-critical bugs                                                            | ≤ **1 business day**  | ≤ **3 business days**  |
| **P4 – Low**      | Cosmetic or minor issues, general questions, content updates                 | ≤ **2 business days** | Next scheduled release |

Examples

P1 Example: “Users unable to search or login”

P2 Example: “About or Weather page down”

P3 Example: “Banner image is not displaying on webpage”

P4 Example: “Update homepage text to reflect a new information”

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Performance Metrics

Technical perfomance
- Website uptime vs exptected 95%
- Page load speed
- Error rate logging/monitoring 

Support perfomance
- Average response time (goal under 4 hours pr ticket)
- Number of incidents by priority
- Solution time for issues(P1 within 8 hours, P2 within 24 hours, P3 within 72 hours, P4 within 1 week)

Delivery and improvements metrics
- Analytics improvements
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. Client Responsibilities

EK agrees to
- Submit accurate descriptions of support requests 
- Designate a single primary contact for communication
- Ensure content, product information, and media assets are accurate and copyright compliant
- Respond to requests for clarification within 3 business days.

Example:
“If Acme needs to update the navigation menu, they must provide correct wording and URLs in the support ticket.”
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Maintenance & Scheduled Downtime
- GitDenGas Notifies EK 48 hours before schedules maintenance
- Maintenance happens monday during 7 AM - 8 AM CET
- expected downtime 10-30 minutes
- Scheduled downtimw is not added to metrics

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. Change Management

8.1 Change Requests (CRs)
Any request outside the defined scope must be submitted formally as a Change Request.

8.2 Change Impact Evaluation
Each CR will include:
- Time estimate
- Cost estimate
- Revised delivery timeline
- Potential risks or dependencies

CRs will have to be accepted by both parties before being implemented
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. Escalation Procedure
If a support issue is not resolved within the expected timeframe:
- Submit ticket
- Level 1 Escalation: Scrummaster
- Level 2 Escalation: Project Manager
- Level 3 Escalation: Operations Lead

Example:
"If search enginge breaks and remains unresolved after p1 timeline, They escalate to Scrummaster."
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to protect business data, analytics, designs, credentials, and trade information.
Confidential information must not be shared with third parties without written consent.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

11. Term & Termination
- This SLA remains in effect from Signed date and 365 days forward, unless renegotiated or replaces with new SLA.
- Immidate termination may occur if
    - A party engages in illegal activities or data misuse
    - Repeated and material breach of SLA obligations after written notice
    - Failure to maintain minimum agreed service levels for 3 consecutive months
    - Breach of confidentiality obligations
